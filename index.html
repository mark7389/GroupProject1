Danger True Believers!

Thanos has imprisoned Earth's Mightiest Heros in the Mirror of Agamotto and scrambled the portal. Can you help our heros by solving the puzzles and stabalizing the portal?

Help return Earth's Mightiest Heros so they can defeat Thanos before he conquers the Earth!

How to Play

You will be given a random puzzle image that has been shuffled. Your goal is to put the pieces together in the correct order to rescue the hero. But don't take too long as you are racing the countdown clock. If the clock reaches zero before you've solved the puzzle, the hero is lost.

Upon the start of the game, you will pick a difficulty setting of Easy, Medium or Hard.

Easy Setting:
You will have 20 seconds to solve a 9-piece puzzle.

Medium Setting:
You will have 30 seconds to solve a 16-piece puzzle.

Hard Setting:
You will have 60 seconds to solve a 25-piece puzzle.

Good Luck!
